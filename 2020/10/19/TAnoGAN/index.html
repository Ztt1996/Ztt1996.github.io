TAnoGAN: Time Series Anomaly Detection with Generative Adversarial Networks

文章下载地址：

https://arxiv.org/pdf/2008.09567.pdf

Overview

在实际生产过程中，例如制造业，医学图像处理，信息安全等领域，对于时间序列的异常检测具有重要的意义。近年来，利用生成对抗网络 (GAN) 进行生成和异常检测在图像处理领域受到了广泛的关注。时间序列的异常检测往往可以看作是一个无监督学习任务。现有的大部分无监督学习异常检测方法，无法处理时间序列间的非线性关系，因此本文提出了一种基于GAN的监督学习方法，用于处理小样本情况下的时间序列异常检测。

Contributions

本文作者总结了如下贡献点：

  1. We propose a GAN-based method called TAnoGan to detect anomalies in time series datasets when a small number of data points are available. In general, neural network-based models require setting a large number of parameters. Therefore, these models need to be trained on huge number of data points.
  2. We evaluate TAnoGan on an extensive and diverse range of 46 time series datasets from the NAB collection that cover avariety of domains.
  3. We show that LSTM-based GAN improves over LSTM by utilising adversarial training in time
    series data.
  4. We show that TAnoGan performs better than traditional and neural network models.

Methodology

前情提要：

  1. 无监督学习方法的异常检测模式

主要考察样本沿时间是否服从一个正常的分布，将不服从分布的样本点视为异常点；

  1. 基于GAN的异常检测的方法的基本模式

Detecting anomalies in time series using GAN requires modelling the normal behaviour of time
series data using the adversarial training process and then detecting anomalies using an anomaly score that indicates how much the data points have deviated from the normal behaviour [3], [12], [2].

For learning the anomaly score, we first map the real time series data space to a latent space and then reconstruct the data from latent space. The anomaly score is estimated as the loss between the real data and reconstructed (fake) data.
